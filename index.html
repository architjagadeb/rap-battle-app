Frequently Asked Questions

How do battles work? +
Battles are turn-based rap competitions where two rappers face off. Each rapper gets 2 minutes per turn to deliver their verses. The community votes for their favorite performance.
How does voting work? +
Anyone can vote for their favorite rapper during a battle. Votes are counted in real-time, and the rapper with the most votes wins the battle.
Can I participate as a spectator? +
Yes! Spectator mode allows you to watch battles, vote for your favorite performers, and engage in the community chat without participating in the battle directly.
What makes BarWars different from other platforms? +
BarWars combines AI-powered voice conversion, real-time battles, and a vibrant community. Our platform offers unique features like voice customization, pre-written verse suggestions, and an interactive voting system.
How does the voice conversion work? +
Our AI technology converts your written verses into dynamic rap vocals. You can choose from various voice styles and customize the delivery to match your style.
How do I improve my ranking? +
Win battles, receive votes, and engage with the community. Your ranking increases based on your performance, consistency, and community engagement.
Are there any rules for battles? +
Yes, we maintain a respectful environment. No hate speech, explicit content, or personal attacks. Focus on creativity, wordplay, and musical talent.
